The Problem

  • Busy professionals lack time for market shopping
  • Elderly residents face mobility challenges
  • Tourists are unfamiliar with local markets and vendors
  • Traditional markets remain essential but can be inaccessible
  • Limited delivery options from local vendors
Market Runner Platform
4/16

Our Solution: Market Runner Platform

A community-driven digital service that:

  • Connects customers to local shoppers ("runners")
  • Facilitates grocery delivery from traditional markets
  • Preserves market culture while adding modern convenience
  • Creates income opportunities
  • Supports local vendors
Market Runner Platform
5/16

How It Works

  1. Customer submits shopping request through website
  2. Local runner accepts the order
  3. Runner purchases items from traditional market vendors
  4. Runner delivers groceries to customer's doorstep
  5. Customer pays through the platform
  6. Platform distributes payment to runner and vendors
